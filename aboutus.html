ABOUT THE COMPANY

BUILDING KENYA SINCE 1974

Shiv Construction Company limited has over forty years’ experience in building and construction industry in Kenya. We are renowned specialists in these areas having worked on housing schemes, Universities & student centres, hospitals, banks, office blocks, factories & civil works. At Shiv Construction we are dedicated to providing infrastructure and building solutions for tomorrow’s world. We combine creative planning, design and construction with innovative engineering techniques to develop the most appropriate solutions, working sustainably to conserve energy, protect the environment and build communities.
